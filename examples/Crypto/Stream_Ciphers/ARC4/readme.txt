Junaid Jameel Ahmad, Shujun Li @ Uni-Konstanz

14 July, 2010

**********************************************************

Due to its simplicity, this CAL implementation of ARC4 contains only one CAL file: ARC4.cal.

ARC4_testbed.XDF is the test bed, which has been tested under both Orcc and OpenDF.
Source_ARC4.cal and Validator_ARC4.cal are used by the test bed to get data and validate the response.
